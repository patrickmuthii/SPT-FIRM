Our Risk Management Framework

Markets are inherently uncertain, but disciplined risk control ensures that uncertainty does not become danger for our investors. Our framework focuses on consistency, capital safety, and resilience across changing conditions.

1. Capital Preservation

  • Every trade has a strict stop-loss.
  • Exposure per trade is capped to protect against volatility shocks.

2. Diversification of Risk

  • Diversified across instruments, timeframes, and market conditions.
  • Performance is never tied to a single market.

3. Monthly Reserve Allocation

  • 20% of income allocated to reserve.
  • Acts as a buffer for unforeseen market conditions.

4. Continuous Monitoring

  • Positions monitored in real time with alerts for volatility.
  • Strict compliance and reporting for transparency.

Why This Matters for Investors

  • Capital safety — growth never pursued recklessly.
  • Resilience — reserves and diversification protect against shocks.
  • Transparency — investors know exactly how capital is protected.

At SPT Capital, our goal is not only to generate returns but also to build trust through responsible, long-term stewardship of investor capital.
