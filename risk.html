Risk Management at SPT Capital

At SPT Capital, risk management is the foundation of everything we do. Markets are inherently uncertain, but disciplined risk control ensures that uncertainty does not become danger for our investors. Our philosophy is simple: protect capital first, then pursue growth.

1. Capital Preservation

  • Every trade is executed with a strict stop-loss, ensuring no single position can compromise the portfolio.
  • Exposure per trade is capped to a small percentage of available capital to protect against volatility shocks.

2. Diversification of Risk

  • Strategies are diversified across instruments, timeframes, and market conditions.
  • Performance is not overly dependent on a single market or trend.

3. Monthly Reserve Allocation

  • 20% of monthly income is allocated to a dedicated reserve account.
  • This reserve acts as a buffer to manage unforeseen market conditions, operational risks, or liquidity demands without affecting investor capital.

4. Continuous Monitoring & Compliance

  • Positions are monitored in real time with automated alerts for volatility or drawdowns.
  • Framework follows strict compliance and reporting standards, ensuring transparency and accountability.

Why This Matters for Investors

  • Capital safety comes first — growth is never pursued recklessly.
  • Resilience against shocks — reserves and diversification protect against unprepared events.
  • Transparency — investors know exactly how their capital is protected.

At SPT Capital, our goal is not only to generate returns but also to build the trust that comes from responsible, long-term stewardship of investor capital.
